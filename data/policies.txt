Hospital Theatre Scheduling Rules (Policy Evidence)

1. Surgeon Qualification Rule:
Reject if the selected surgeon is not qualified to perform the selected operation.

2. Equipment Rule:
Reject if the selected theatre does not contain every equipment item required by the selected operation.

3. Theatre Compatibility Rule:
Reject if the theatre type is not compatible with the operation specialty.

4. Time Conflict Rule:
Reject if the selected surgeon or theatre has an overlapping booking in the requested time range.

5. ASAP Scheduling Rule:
For ASAP scheduling, select the earliest time slot in the search window that satisfies rules 1 to 4.
